Sean Mackey

I am a software engineer with a background in booking and managing theatrical tours of Broadway shows. I reliably navigated the copious moving parts of that process with a passion for details, organization, accuracy, and delivering on my word, and those commitments inform my work as a developer.

I have experience with Ruby, Rails, JavaScript, React, Redux, Node.js, SQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Express, Git, AWS, HTML5, CSS3, and Heroku.

In my free time you can find me at the New York Philharmonic, whipping up new recipes in the kitchen, lifting weights, traveling, and working hard at being the favorite uncle to my nieces and nephews.
